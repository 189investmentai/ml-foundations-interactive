Questions

Who is this for?

Decision Makers is for PMs, analysts, and leaders who work with ML teams but don't build models. Practitioners is for engineers and data scientists who build and deploy models. Pick the curriculum that matches your role.

Do I need to know Python?

Decision Makers: No. The labs use Colab with guided code—you can run and tweak without prior Python experience. Practitioners: Yes. Comfort with Python and pandas is expected.

What tools do I need?

Just a Google account. Everything runs in Colab (free tier). No local setup, Docker, or cloud accounts required.

How long does it take?

Decision Makers: ~60-90 min per module, roughly 15-20 hours total. Practitioners: ~30-45 min per module, roughly 15-20 hours. Both are self-paced.

Is there support?

This is a fully async, one-time-purchase product. No live office hours or community. You get comprehensive answer keys, rubrics, and troubleshooting docs for every exercise.

Ready to start learning?

Try a free module from either curriculum. No account required—just open the link and go.
