📖 Metric Reference Show ▼
📏 MAE: Average |error|. Robust to outliers. In original units.
📐 RMSE: √(avg error²). Penalizes big errors. In original units.
📊 R²: 1 - (your error / baseline). 0-1 scale, higher = better.
💯 MAPE: Average % error. Good for comparing scales.
🎯 Residual: Actual - Predicted. Should be random around 0.
⚠️ RMSE >> MAE: You have outlier errors. Investigate extremes.
