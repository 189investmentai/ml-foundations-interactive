How to Read This Show
🔵/🔴 Points: Blue = Retained, Red = Churned.
🟦/🟥 Regions: Colored areas show what the tree predicts in that zone.
🟡 Yellow ring: Misclassified points (prediction != actual).
📊 Train vs Test: If train >> test, the tree is memorizing noise.
🌳 Depth: More depth = more splits = more complex boundaries.
🍃 Leaves: Final prediction boxes. More leaves = more memorization risk.
