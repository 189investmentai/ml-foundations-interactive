📖 Optimizer Reference Show ▼
🚶 SGD: Follow gradient directly. Simple but can be slow.
🏃 Momentum: Add velocity from past steps. Accelerates convergence.
📏 RMSProp: Adapt LR per dimension. Good for different scales.
⭐ Adam: Momentum + RMSProp. The default choice.
📈 High LR: Large steps. Fast but can overshoot/diverge.
📉 Low LR: Small steps. Stable but slow convergence.
🏔️ Ravine: Curvature differs across dimensions - causes oscillation. Momentum and Adam handle this well.
🪑 Saddle point: Gradient near zero but not a minimum. Adam's adaptive LR escapes faster.
